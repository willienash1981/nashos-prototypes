The scope described in this proposal represents the complete work. Any changes require a formal change order.

Color Selection Disclaimer

Final color selections are your responsibility. We recommend viewing colors in natural light and using a Color Muse tool. Color changes after painting begins may incur charges.

Change Orders

Any additions or changes must be authorized in writing via the NashOS change order process. May affect timeline and cost.

Warranty

Workmanship is warranted per the scope above. Warranty activation requires completion of a post-project survey. See Warranty PDF for full terms.

Licensing & Insurance

Nash Painting is fully licensed and insured in Tennessee. Certificates available upon request.

Subcontractors

Nash Painting may use qualified subcontractors for specialty work. All carry their own insurance.

Right to Cancel

You may cancel within 3 business days of acceptance for a full refund.

Dispute Resolution

Disputes resolved through good-faith negotiation, then binding arbitration in Davidson County, Tennessee.
