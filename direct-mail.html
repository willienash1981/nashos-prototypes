📋 How Postcards Fit Into Campaigns
The Campaign Builder (§6) defines the full customer journey — WHEN to send and WHO receives it. Direct Mail Manager handles HOW — printing, mailing, photos, and tracking. Postcards are just another channel step alongside email, SMS, and voicemail drop.

🔧 Campaign Builder Owns

  • Trigger events (job complete, estimate sent, etc.)
  • Timing & sequencing (Day 1, Day 4, Day 15…)
  • Audience targeting & segmentation
  • Conditional logic (IF no review → send card)
  • Channel selection per step
  • Suppression & blacklist enforcement

📬 Direct Mail Manager Owns

  • Template design & postcard rendering
  • Photo sourcing (Hero → AFTER → CompanyCam)
  • Print fulfillment (Lob / PostGrid)
  • Tracking codes (NP-XXXX) per card type
  • QR code generation
  • Delivery tracking & return handling
  • Neighbor Reach (non-customer targeting)

"We just painted a home on Granny White Pike!"

"We just completed a beautiful exterior painting on {{job.streetName}} in {{job.neighborhood}}. Your neighbors chose Nashville's most-reviewed painters — {{company.reviewCount}} 5-star reviews."
