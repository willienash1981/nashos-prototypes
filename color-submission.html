✨ Understanding Paint Sheens ▼
Flat / Matte
No shine, hides imperfections. Best for ceilings & low-traffic walls.
Eggshell
Subtle warmth, slight sheen. Best for living rooms, bedrooms, hallways.
Satin
Smooth, velvety, easy to clean. Best for kitchens, bathrooms, kids' rooms.
Semi-Gloss
Noticeable shine, very durable. Best for trim, doors, cabinets, baseboards.

⚠️ Higher sheen = more visible surface imperfections
