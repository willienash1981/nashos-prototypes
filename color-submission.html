💡 Quick Guide: What is Sheen?
Sheen is how shiny the paint finish looks — it's separate from color. The same color can look very different depending on the sheen.

🎨 Flat — No shine. Hides imperfections and dings. Easy to touch up — small scuffs disappear. Our top choice for walls and ceilings.

🥚 Eggshell — A soft, light shimmer. Gives a warm glow but touch-ups may show. Nice if you want just a hint of sheen on your walls.

🧈 Satin — Smooth and easy to wipe clean. The modern standard for trim, doors, cabinets, kitchens, and bathrooms. Touch-ups will be visible.

✨ Semi-Gloss — Noticeable shine. Very durable. A traditional choice for trim and doors — touch-ups will show. Some clients prefer this classic look.

⚠️ Higher sheen = more visible imperfections and harder touch-ups. We recommend Flat for most walls — it's the easiest to maintain.
